MPT1113 Educational Technology Foundation

This is a foundation course to educational technology, information and communication technology (ICT), basic principles of instructional design and multimedia development and e-learning, together with computer-based information system management. Among aspects that will be discussed are definition of educational technology (process & product), current development of ICT and its impact toward teaching, learning and management, several instructional design models, knowledge management, ergonomic, research and development relate with information technology applications in education.

MPT1193 Authoring System

This course will expose students with basic programming and authoring concept. Aspects that will be discussed are introduction to programming, programming language, and authoring system. This course also will give opportunity to students to analyze programming and authoring skills of several languages. The students will also be exposed to basic web programming skills and concepts using HTML and then move to complex concepts suchas the integration of server-side scripting languages such as Active Server Pages (ASP) and PHP and also the use of databases such as Microsoft Access & MySQL. Through this course students will be given opportunity to discover knowledge and skills to develop static and dynamic webpage and distinguish the differences between ASP and PHP, Microsoft Access and MySQL, and Internet Information Services and Apache.

MPT 1293 (CD-ROM Based Multimedia Development)

This course gives exposure and experience to students in developing interactive educational multimedia software based on any software design theory. Aspects that will be discussed in this course are project management approaches, development process and final format of the software. Several evaluation approaches and software applications in teaching and learning will be conducted in this course. This course requires authoring skills that have been learned in MPT 1193.
