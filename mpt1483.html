MPT1483 Visual Technology Foundation

This course gives exposures on theories and skills of design and production of visual static media. Students will develop a complete package using the media. The aspect of computer integration will be focused more in this course. Students also will evaluate the package that has been developed. The production of static media will focus more on photography and digital graphics fields in teaching. Varieties of photographing techniques, development of visual materials and image manipulation techniques will be focused in this course to produce excellent and quality teaching materials. A pre-requisite of this course is MPT 1183: Instructional Material Production.
